Launching Easily and Compliantly

From planning and design to compliance and operational setup, there are a number of important phases involved in starting a laboratory. Through a partnership with Dynamic IT Healthcare Lab Start up services, you can guarantee a seamless and legal laboratory start, laying the groundwork for future success.

Planning and Design
Assessment
  1. Objective Setting: Define the purpose and scope of the laboratory.
  2. Space Requirements: Evaluate space based on the type of lab (e.g., clinical, research, industrial).
  3. Budget Planning: Estimate initial costs and operational expenses.
Design Services
  1. Architectural Design: Layout planning to ensure efficient workflow.
  2. Engineering Services: Design of electrical, plumbing, HVAC, and safety systems.
  3. Interior Design: Selection of materials and finishes that comply with safety regulations and are suitable for laboratory environments.
Regulatory Compliance
Licensing and Permits
  1. Local and Federal Permits: Obtain necessary permits based on the lab’s location and type.
  2. Regulatory Agencies: Ensure compliance with agencies like CLIA, CAP, and COLA.
Safety Standards
  1. Biosafety Levels: Implementation of biosafety measures appropriate to the type of work being conducted (BSL-1 to BSL-4).
  2. Chemical Safety: Proper storage and handling of chemicals, with adherence to MSDS guidelines.
  3. Fire Safety: Installation of fire suppression systems and compliance with fire codes.
Equipment and Supplies
Procurement Services
  1. Vendor Selection: Define the purpose and scope of the laboratory.
  2. Equipment Installation: Evaluate space based on the type of lab (e.g., clinical, research, industrial).
  3. Supply Chain Management: Estimate initial costs and operational expenses.
Data Management Systems
  1. LIMS: Implementation of Laboratory Information Management Systems for data tracking and management.
  2. IT Infrastructure: Setting up network systems, computers, and secure data storage solutions.
  3. Software Solutions: Selection and installation of necessary software for lab operations, data analysis, and reporting.
Staffing and Training
Recruitment Services
  1. Hiring: Recruiting qualified staff, including scientists, technicians, and administrative personnel.
  2. Credential Verification: Ensuring all staff credentials and certifications are up to date.
Training Programs
  1. Operational Training: Training staff on standard operating procedures and equipment use.
  2. Safety Training: Comprehensive training on lab safety protocols, emergency procedures, and compliance with regulatory standards.
Operational Setup
Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)
  1. Documentation: Development of SOPs for all laboratory processes.
  2. Quality Control: Establishing quality control measures to maintain high standards of lab work.
Maintenance and Calibration
  1. Routine Maintenance: Setting schedules for regular maintenance of equipment.
  2. Calibration Services: Regular calibration of instruments to ensure accuracy and reliability.
Consulting Services
Consulting Services
  1. Operational Review: Periodic review of laboratory operations to identify areas for improvement.
  2. Compliance Audits: Regular audits to ensure continued compliance with all regulatory requirements.
Expansion and Innovation
  1. Growth Planning: Strategizing for future expansion and new service offerings.
  2. Innovation: Keeping up with the latest technological advancements and integrating them into lab operations.
